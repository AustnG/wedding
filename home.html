Our Story


Ashley and Austin began dating around 2015. They first met at a birthday party for their mutual friend Duncan McKenzie.

Thru the following months they kept in touch and continued to see each other around other social events in Bowling Green, KY. On New Years Eve of 2015 - at a Pizza Riot show @Rocky's Bar - Ashley asked Austin for his number. They began dating shortly after.

During their time dating they've enjoyed various hobbies together such as cooking, traveling, attending concerts, and even DJing! It's common place to see the two taking the stage together to DJ for evenings of sick ass tunes that really make the 21 year olds wish there was a younger DJ.
